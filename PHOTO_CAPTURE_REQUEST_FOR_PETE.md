## Why We Need These

The new quoting system lets customers and salespeople select products by clicking on image cards —
like shopping online. Every product card needs a photo. Where we have no photo, we'll use a
placeholder, but real photos look much better and help sell the product.

---

## What Makes a Good Photo

- Outdoors in good light (overcast is ideal — no harsh shadows)
- 3/4 angle from front-left shows the most detail
- Body or trailer on its own OR on a truck (both are useful)
- Horizontal orientation (landscape)
- Camera or phone is fine — just make sure it's in focus
- Minimum width: 1200px (modern phones are fine)
